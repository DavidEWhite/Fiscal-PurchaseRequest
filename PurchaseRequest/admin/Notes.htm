Notes Regarding Staff Records

Add a person as a recipient of the message
Step 1 – Add the person to Restricted Access, choosing “Fiscal” for the role
Step 2 – Add the new person’s email using the option now available under Approvers, in the Admin area

Remove a person as a recipient
If the person no longer needs email, but needs access to the application …
Go to Approvers and remove the email address for the person displayed under "Fiscal Approval"

If the person no longer needs access at all …
Go to Restricted Access and, next to the person desired, choose DELETE
